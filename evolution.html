Evolution of Memes

Initially, memes referred to fashion trends, catchphrases, and rituals. However, with the advent of the internet, memes took on a new form as they migrated into the digital realm. With the proliferation of smartphones and continuous expansion of the digital landscape, memes have become an integral part of online communication, reflecting and shaping contemporary culture in profound ways. Today, memes are an integral part of internet culture, serving as a form of expression and humor for millions of users worldwide. They reflect current events, and societal norms, resonating with people from all walks of life. As the internet continues to evolve, so too will the nature and impact of memes, shaping the way we communicate and interact online.
